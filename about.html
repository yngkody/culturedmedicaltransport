Our Story

Cultured Medical Transport was born from a profound personal journey. For over ten years, our founder personally managed the complex medical transportation needs of a close family member. This lived experience exposed the critical gaps in the industry: the need for punctuality, empathy, and absolute patient dignity.

"We don't just transport patients; we serve neighbors with the same meticulous care we provided our own family for a decade."
