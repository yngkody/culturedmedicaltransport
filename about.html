Our Story

Cultured Medical Transport was founded on lived experience, operational discipline, and a deep respect for patient dignity. For over a decade, we personally transported and cared for a dialysis-dependent family member multiple times per week. We witnessed firsthand how critical punctual, reliable, and respectful transportation is to a patient’s health and quality of life.

"We don't just transport patients; we serve neighbors with the same meticulous care we provided our own family."
